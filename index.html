About me

Hello! I'm Ganesh Doosa, a passionate Senior Data Scientist, AI Solution Architect, and Machine Learning Engineer from Bangalore, India. With 10+ years of expertise in AI, software development, and designing machine learning systems across Retail, IoT, and Telecom industries, I specialize in business problem-solving, data analysis, Generative AI, and ML/DL model building.

I excel at architecting end-to-end AI systems and deploying production-ready solutions that drive business success. My work spans from conceptualization to deployment, building scalable ML pipelines, designing intelligent applications, and implementing enterprise-grade AI solutions. I'm particularly excited about leveraging cutting-edge Generative AI technologies to solve complex business challenges.

Armed with a Master's degree in Machine Learning and AI from LJMU University and PGD from IIIT-Bangalore, I combine academic rigor with practical expertise. When I'm not crafting algorithms or designing AI architectures, you'll find me exploring new technologies, capturing moments through photography, or enjoying my favorite peanut candy! ❤
